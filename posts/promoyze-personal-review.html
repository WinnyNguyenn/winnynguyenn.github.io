How Does It Work?

Promoyze Personal is designed for ultimate simplicity and productivity. Here’s how it works:

Step 1: Choose a Template
You start by browsing through the library of 100+ professional video templates. These are organized by niche, business type, and style.

Step 2: Click and Swap
Once you select a template, just click on the text, images, or video placeholders and replace them with your own content. The built-in editor is smooth and beginner-friendly.

Step 3: Render and Export
After customizing your video, simply hit render. The software takes care of transitions, animations, and formatting. The final video is ready to be published on any platform.

There’s no need to manually adjust keyframes, layer effects, or audio timing. Everything is baked into the template, making the entire process fast and frustration-free.
