How Does It Work?

  1. Log in to your dashboard
    After registering, users access a streamlined dashboard where they can choose their preferred content type – eBook, video, coloring book, or flipbook.

  2. Select a niche or input your own idea
    Choose from 10 popular niches or enter a custom idea. The platform uses AI to process your input and begin creating a script or storyline.

  3. Let the AI build your content
    Visuals, text, voiceover, and layout are automatically generated. Users can preview and make manual edits to suit their specific needs.

  4. Customize your slides or pages
    Add animations, effects, text overlays, or voiceover changes as needed. This step is optional but useful for creators wanting a more polished output.

  5. Download and monetize
    Final products are available in MP4, PDF, and flipbook formats. You can then upload your content to YouTube, Amazon KDP, Etsy, or use it for your own educational business.
